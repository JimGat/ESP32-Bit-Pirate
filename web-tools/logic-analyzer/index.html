What it does

      
Capture digital signals from SUMP-compatible logic analyzer firmware, then inspect timing, triggers and exported waveforms in the browser.

    

    

      
Adapter

      
    
        
  • Use a SUMP-compatible device or firmware exposed as a serial port.
    • 
        
  • Connect the analyzer ground to the target ground before connecting signal channels.
    • 
        
  • Keep input voltages within the analyzer hardware limits.
